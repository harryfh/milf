Why MILF?

Muarane, MILF of the Year (2023)

The MILF of the Year

We aim to showcase the finest films, and create an unforgettable experience for our audience. We are so proud of the submissions from this Past Milf. We are thrilled to announce Muarane as our Milf of the Year. Her exceptional talent and contribution with her film Duct Tape added to this years MILF-iness

A Stunning Venue

Join us at the Cabaret Lion d'Or, conveniently located at 1676, rue Ontario est, Montréal (Québec), as we honor Muarane and pay tribute to her remarkable career. Prepare to be immersed in the magic of cinema, surrounded by fellow film lovers and industry professionals.

A Different Type of Party

Milf Fest is not just about films. Join us for a diverse celebration of music, films, and custom apparel. Experience the talents of Chromatones Accapella group and local Montreal DJs.
