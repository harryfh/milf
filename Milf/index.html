What is MILF?

MILF Fest is an event based in Montreal and focused on showcasing short films and providing a platform for filmmakers to present their work.

In the past three years, we've received over 2000 submissions from all of the world. This year, we selected the top 20 short films for the festival.

On January 21st, come see our selection at Cabaret Lion d'Or. Tickets available here.

More than a Film Festival



Formerly organized by a tiny film club at McGill University, MILF Fest understands the value in young talent who want to pursue their dreams of filmmaking.

MILF Fest has also been a platform for others to showcase all kinds of art. Throughout the years, we've showcased artists such as painters, singers, drag artists and more!

MILF Fest 2023 was locateed at Cabaret Lion d'Or

We reward artists

Participants may partake in different categories such as: Drama, Comedy, Horror, and Experimental.

This year, there are five films in each category. The audience will have the opportunity to vote and award the winning filmmakers with a prize.
